Hello, I am a designer, coder, circuitry seamstress, and crafter living in Brooklyn, NY.

I am a graphic designer turned UX researcher. I recently earned my Masters from NYU ITP, where I studied interaction design, programming, and physical computing. My primary focus was on the intersection of emerging tech and traditional craft through wearable tech, soft circuitry, and app design.

I am passionate about designing and developing both digital and physical interfaces, and conducting research that can inspire meaningful experiences. I am a self-motivated learner with a love for teaching and hosting workshops whenever I can.
